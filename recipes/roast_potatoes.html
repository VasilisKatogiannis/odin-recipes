Crunchy Roast Potatoes

Description

Oven-baked potatoes in a unique recipe, where the crispness of the result is incredible.

Ingredients

  • 1 kilo potatoes
  • 2 lemons
  • 1 1/2 liter water
  • 1-2 tablespoon(s) thyme, fresh
  • 2 clove(s) of garlic
  • 70 g olive oil
  • 30 g semolina, fine
  • salt
  • pepper
  • 1 teaspoon(s) paprika, sweet
  • chicken burger patties, to serve

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 200* C (390* F) Fan.
  2. Place the baking pan you choose to use in the oven.
  3. Score each piece with a knife in 2-3 places.
  4. Transfer to a pot and add the juice of 2 lemons plus the actual squeezed lemons used.
  5. Add salt, pepper and 1 Â½ liters of water and bring to a boil.
  6. Boil for 10 minutes, until the potatoes soften a little.
  7. When ready, drain, remove the lemons and allow all of the steam to evaporate from the potatoes. In this way they will release all of the moisture.
  8. In a bowl, add the potatoes, olive oil, thyme, whole garlic cloves, paprika, semolina, salt and pepper.
  9. Shake the bowl to combine all of the ingredients.
  10. Carefully remove the hot baking pan from the oven and immediately add the potatoes.
  11. Spread them out and quickly place the baking pan back into the oven.
  12. Bake for 30-40 minutes.
  13. Turn the potatoes every 10 minutes so that they can become golden evenly.
  14. When ready, remove from oven and serve.
